# Essential Guidelines

## Intubation

### [Guidelines for the management of tracheal intubation in critically ill adults](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.bja.2017.10.021)

Difficult Airway Society guidelines for intubation in critically ill adults including pre-intubation checklist and failed intubation algorithm.

## Emergencies

### [AAGBI Quick Reference Handbook](https://anaesthetists.org/Guidelines-and-publications/Quick-reference-handbook/PDF-version)

Anaesthetics crises management algorithms and checklists.

## Conditions

### [Surviving Sepsis Campaign: International Guidelines for Management of Sepsis and Septic Shock 2021](https://doi.org/10.1097/CCM.0000000000005337)

Latest surviving sepsis recommendations. 

### [ESICM guidelines on acute respiratory distress syndrome: definition, phenotyping and respiratory support strategies](https://doi.org/10.1007/s00134-023-07050-7)

Latest ARDS management guidelines.

### [European Resuscitation Council and European Society of Intensive Care Medicine Guidelines 2025 Post-Resuscitation Care](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.resuscitation.2025.110809)

Latests post-resuscitation care guidelines including physiological targets and neuro-prognostication algorithm.

### [TOXBASE](https://www.toxbase.org)

National Poisons Information Service database.
